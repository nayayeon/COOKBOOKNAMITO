Filipino Recipes

Boneless Bangus

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless milkfish (Bangus)
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the boneless Bangus in vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry the marinated Bangus until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce.

Adobong Manok

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Brown the chicken in oil, then add the marinade, vinegar, bay leaf, peppercorns, and water.
  3. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until chicken is tender.
  4. Adjust seasoning and serve with rice.

Lumpia

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg ground pork or beef
  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup green beans, finely chopped
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix meat, vegetables, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place a tablespoon of the mixture in a wrapper and roll tightly.
  3. Fry in oil until golden brown and serve with dipping sauce.

  • 1 cup okra, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, chopped
